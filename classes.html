Our Classes and Curriculum

Nursery Class Curriculum: Basic Qur’an and Arabic

Class Teacher: Mallam Ishaq Kabir

This is the entry-level class at our Islamiyya. Students are introduced to basic Qur’an reading, daily Islamic supplications, and simple Hadith.

Primary Class Curriculum: Qur’an, Tajweed, Islamic Studies

Class Teacher: Mallam Mustapha Isah

The students here are third in the class hierarchy. They learn Qur’an recitation, Tajweed, Hadith, and foundational Islamic knowledge.

Secondary Class Curriculum: Tafsir, Fiqh, Hadith

Class Teacher: Mallam Ilyas Kusherki

Ranked second in the hierarchy, this class dives deeper into Tafsir (Qur’anic exegesis), Hadith, Fiqh (Islamic jurisprudence), and advanced Qur’an recitation.

Advanced Class Curriculum: Seerah, Fiqh, Aqeedah

Class Teacher: Mallam Aliyu Dabban

This is the highest-level class in our Islamiyya. Students study Seerah (Prophetic biography), advanced Fiqh, Aqeedah (Islamic belief), Hadith, and Tafsir.
