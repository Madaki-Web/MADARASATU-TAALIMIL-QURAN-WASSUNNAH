About Our Islamiyya

Madarasatul-Ta,alimil Quran Was-sunnah is an esteemed Islamic educational Institue
dedicated to providing high quality education rooted in the principles
of Quran and sunnah, with the aim to nurture a love of learning
, spiritual growth, and moral character in our students
founded to provide Islamic knowledge and Qur’anic education to students of all ages. Over the years, we have nurtured students who are now contributing positively to society.

Progress & Needs

We continue to expand with more classes, better facilities, and dedicated teachers. We still seek community support to grow further and achieve our vision.
